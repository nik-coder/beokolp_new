ABOUT THE COMPANY

Beokolp Ltd. Belgrade is a company in Serbia that deals with the distribution of printed media, books, CD and DVD. The company was founded in 1997, from its inception to the day we have achieved that in the overall print market, at the national level, we are one of the largest distributors.
We place all editions in more than 8400 retail stores on the territory of the Republic of Serbia. We supply both small trade shops and large systems such as Mercator, Corner kiosk, Amman, Domestic Trade, Dufry, Gomex, Inmedio, Univerexport, petrol stations NIS, OMV, Eco Srbija, Knez Petrol, MOL, BS Petrol, mozzart betting shops...
We are exporting the Serbian press to the territory of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Macedonia, Kosovo and Metohija.
More than two decades of experience in the print distribution market has helped us expand our business in the part of the sale of various consumer goods of well-known brands (Orbit, Gillette, Nes cafe) as well as imports of condiment products from Spain, Italy, Poland, Slovakia, Turkey, Greece, Russia and Ukraine.
We have a fleet of over 180 delivery vehicles that deliver printing and other goods to MP facilities on the territory of the entire Republic of Serbia.
We have distribution centers in all major cities in the Republic of Serbia and the region that guarantee fast and efficient delivery of printing and other goods.
